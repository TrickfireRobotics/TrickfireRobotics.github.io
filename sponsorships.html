SPONSORSHIPS

Sponsor Us!

TrickFire relies on generous donations to purchase the necessary materials and equipment to build the rover and travel to competitions. Your donations will directly impact the development of our students the team's opportunities. In-kind sponsorships are also a tax-favored solution to help our team get much-needed resources and tools to help construct the robot.

In addition to donations, we seek industry mentors. Our team strives for professional quality work. As such, working with your organization’s professionals provides invaluable experience that helps us reach that goal while giving you a direct connection to candidate employees.
