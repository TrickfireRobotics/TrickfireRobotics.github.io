SPONSORSHIPS

Sponsor Us!

TrickFire Robotics relies on generous donation to purchase necessary materials and equipment to build the rover and travel to competitions. Your donations will directly impact the development of our students. In-kind sponsorships are also a tax-favored solution to help our team get much-needed resources and or tools to help construct the rover.

In addition to donations, we seek industry mentors. Our team strives for professional quality work. Working with your organization’s professionals provides invaluable experience that helps us reach that goal while giving you a direct connection to candidate employees.

How to Sponsor
