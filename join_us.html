Want to Join Us?

TrickFire’s mission is to give us students hands-on, relevant experiences crucial to entering the working world. Here, you can gain skills in software, mechanical, and electrical engineering, business, and marketing. For example, you can work on a robotic arm, a sponsorship packet, and a drone all in the same club if you’re willing to put in the time. All of these learning opportunities and experiences will improve your resume, give you great talking points, and create fond memories!

No previous experience is needed!
