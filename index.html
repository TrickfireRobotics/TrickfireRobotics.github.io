UW Bothell's Premiere Robotics Team

TrickFire Robotics is a student-led robotics team focused on providing a unique educational experience through hands-on work on large scale projects. TrickFire originally competed in the NASA Lunabotics RMC from 2016 to 2021 and now competes in the University Rover Challenge hosted by The Mars Society.

Our Robot

Our current rover, Viator, is set to participate in the University Rover Challenge. Through iterative development, our robot will be capable of helping astronauts accomplish a variety of tasks.

Our Goals

We will help students develop their technical skills through hands-on experience with different fields of engineering, life detection, finance, and marketing

We will help students develop professional skills by working with a large-scale team of diverse students of different majors and backgrounds.

We will foster a strong and inclusive community at the University of Washington Bothell by creating opportunities for students to engage, collaborate, and support each other
